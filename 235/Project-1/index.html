After cats have inevitably taken over the world, you are forced to run a cafe for cats!

Now that cats have take over the world it is up to you to serve your new fuzzy and impatient overlords at the Cafe Cat. Encounter a variety of different customers with their own quirks and traits. Serve several different dricks and dishes with a disgusting feline twist. All of this and more in Cafe Cat.

Influencer Cat

Player

This item will double the amount of energy Master Higgins receives from energy fruits.

Milk is very good for growing young boys like Master Higgins. Find it and fill up his energy level.
